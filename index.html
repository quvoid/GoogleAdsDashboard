What we measure

📋 How this alert works

What we measure

  • Compares the conversion rate (conversions ÷ clicks) over last 14 days vs previous 14 days
  • Only campaigns with at least 10 clicks previously AND a previous conv rate ≥ 0.5% are included
  • We look at the absolute % change — a drop from 4% to 0% = 100% change

Severity thresholds

  • Critical — rate changed by 75% or more
  • High — 50% to 74% change
  • Warning — 25% to 49% change
  • Watch — 15% to 24% change

Signal Confidence

  • High confidence — campaign had 500+ clicks previously. Trust this alert.
  • Moderate — 100 to 499 clicks. Investigate before acting.
  • Low — under 100 clicks. Likely noise, verify manually.
Impact score = previous clicks × |change %| × type multiplier (Exact match gets 1.4× boost)

📋 How this alert works

What we measure

  • CPC spike: how much more expensive each click has become vs the previous period
  • CTR drop: how much the click-through rate has fallen vs the previous period
  • Both compared over the last 14 days vs the 14 days before

Severity thresholds

  • Critical — CPC spiked ≥10% AND CTR dropped ≥10% (both signals triggered)
  • Warning — CPC spiked ≥10% OR CTR dropped ≥10% (one signal triggered)
  • Normal — neither condition met, campaign never reaches this sheet

Email & ranking

  • Top 5 by impact score are sent in the email
  • Phrase match campaigns get a 1.2× boost in ranking
  • Competitor campaigns get a 0.6× reduction (less urgent)
  • Discovery/Display get 0.5× reduction
Impact score = current spend × severity multiplier × campaign type multiplier

  • High — dropped 50% to 74%
  • Warning — dropped 25% to 49%
  • Watch — dropped 10% to 24%

  • Brand campaigns get 1.8× boost — losing impression share on brand is more urgent
  • Discovery/Display get 0.5× reduction

📋 How this alert works

What we measure

  • Compares total spend in the last 7 days vs the 7 days before that
  • Only campaigns that spent at least ₹500 in the previous period are included
  • New campaigns (prev spend <₹500) are excluded to avoid false alerts

Severity thresholds

  • Critical — spend doubled (increased 100% or more)
  • High — increased 50% to 99%
  • Warning — increased 20% to 49%

Important note

  • This alert is fully independent — a campaign can appear here AND in any quality alert (CPC, Conv Drop, etc.)
  • Top 5 by impact score are sent in the email
Impact score = current 7-day spend × increase % (no type multiplier for spend alerts)
